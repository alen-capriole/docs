## Market Coverage

### Digital Assets
- **Bitcoin**: 15+ proprietary on-chain indicators including Hash Ribbons, Energy Value, Dynamic Range NVT, and Production Cost
- **Ethereum**: On-chain metrics, valuation models, and network activity indicators
- **Altcoins**: Market breadth, speculation indices, and comparative analysis tools
- **Bitcoin Treasuries**: Institutional holdings tracking for 50+ publicly-traded companies

### Macro Markets
- **Equities**: S&P 500 analysis, market breadth, sentiment indicators, and insider activity
- **Fixed Income**: Treasury yields, credit spreads, and bond market analytics
- **Commodities**: Gold, energy, and commodity ratios against equities
- **Currencies**: USD positioning and major currency pair analysis
- **Liquidity Metrics**: Global M2, US liquidity, and central bank balance sheet tracking

### Trading Strategies
- **Macro Index**: Machine learning model combining 100+ fundamental indicators
- **Spectrum**: Pure momentum strategy using price action and volume
- **Node**: On-chain fundamental strategy for long/short positioning
- **Midas**: Gold/USD systematic trading strategy
