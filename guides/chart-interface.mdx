## TradingView Advanced Charts

Capriole Charts uses **TradingView Advanced Charts**, the professional-grade charting library trusted by financial platforms worldwide. If you're familiar with TradingView, you'll feel right at home.

## Default Settings

When you open any chart, these settings are automatically applied:

- **Default timeframe**: 1 Day (1D)
- **Default visible range**: 60 months
- **Logarithmic scale**: Enabled by default for long-term visualization
- **Supported timeframes**: 1D, 1W, 1M
- **Auto-resize**: Charts automatically fit your screen

<Note>
  **Capriole on-chain and macro indicators update daily.** We recommend using **1D or higher timeframes** for accurate data visualization.
</Note>
