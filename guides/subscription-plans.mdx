## Subscription Tiers

Capriole Charts offers three subscription tiers designed for different analytical requirements:

### Premium - $490/month
Complete access to all 100+ indicators, unlimited alert creation, quantitative trading strategies (Macro Index, Node, Spectrum, Midas), Bitcoin Treasury Company analysis tools, Capriole Intel AI research assistant, Guardian exchange risk monitoring, and dedicated support.

**Ideal for**: Professional traders, funds, and institutions

### Pro - $29/month
Full access to 60+ premium indicators, advanced alert system (up to 10 alerts), AI Briefing with daily market analysis, priority support, and extended historical data.

**Ideal for**: Active traders and serious investors

### Free - $0/month
Access to 20+ core indicators, basic charting functionality, dashboard view with market overview, and community support.

**Ideal for**: Casual investors and those new to on-chain analysis

## Frequently Asked Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can I switch plans?">
    Yes. You can upgrade or downgrade at any time. Upgrading provides immediate access with pro-rated billing. Downgrading takes effect at the end of your current billing period. Your saved layouts, alerts, and settings are preserved when changing plans.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="What happens if I cancel?">
    You maintain access until the end of your paid period. Saved chart layouts and settings are preserved. Alerts will be disabled but saved. You can reactivate at any time. Your account reverts to Free tier features after the cancellation period ends.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Is there a free trial?">
    Yes. New users receive a 7-day free trial of Pro features with no credit card required. You have full access to Pro indicators and alerts, with an automatic downgrade to Free tier if not upgraded.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Can I pay annually?">
    Yes. Annual subscriptions include a 20% discount (2 months free). Pro annual: $278/year (save $70). Premium annual: $4,704/year (save $1,176).
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="What payment methods are accepted?">
    We accept credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), cryptocurrency (Bitcoin, Ethereum, USDC, USDT), and wire transfer for annual subscriptions over $500.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="How do I access Premium indicators?">
    Premium indicators appear in the sidebar with badge tags. Clicking a restricted indicator shows an upgrade prompt if you're not subscribed to the required tier.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Can I share my account?">
    Account sharing is not permitted under our Terms of Service. Each user requires their own subscription. For teams and institutions, contact enterprise@capriole.com about multi-user plans.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="What is your refund policy?">
    We offer a 7-day money-back guarantee for new Pro/Premium subscriptions. No refunds for renewals (cancel before next billing cycle). Pro-rated refunds are considered on a case-by-case basis.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Do you offer enterprise plans?">
    Yes. For institutions, hedge funds, and asset managers requiring multi-user access, custom indicators, white-label solutions, dedicated API access, or custom data integration, contact enterprise@capriole.com.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
