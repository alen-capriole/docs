## Workspace

**Workspace** is your personal chart canvas for custom analysis. Access it from the left sidebar menu.

### Features

- Add any combination of Capriole indicators
- Add TradingView built-in indicators (via fx icon)
- Draw trend lines and annotations
- Configure timeframes and chart settings
- Adjust pane layouts

### Saving Your Setup

Click the **Save** button (floppy disk icon) in the top-right toolbar to preserve your workspace configuration.

Your saved layout will:
- Store all indicators and settings
- Sync across all your devices
- Load instantly when you return

<Note>
  Workspace is perfect for building your own analysis dashboard without restrictions.
</Note>
