## Account Setup

### Registration

Navigate to [capriole.com/charts](https://capriole.com/charts) and create an account to begin accessing the platform.

### Subscription Selection

Choose a subscription plan based on your requirements:

- **Free Plan**: Access basic indicators and standard charting features
- **Pro Plan**: Unlock advanced indicators and Guardian risk management tools
- **Premium Plan**: Gain real-time data access and intelligent alert capabilities

## Platform Navigation

### Chart Selection

The platform organizes indicators into logical categories accessible via the left sidebar:

- Strategies
- Quantum Index  
- Bitcoin On-Chain
- Bitcoin Treasuries
- Ethereum
- Market Sentiment
- Macro Economics
- Altcoins
- Guardian Risk Management

### Chart Interface

The main chart area utilizes TradingView's professional charting library with full customization capabilities.
