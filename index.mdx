## Platform Overview

Capriole Charts is a professional financial data visualization platform providing institutional-grade market analysis tools. Our platform combines over 100 proprietary indicators across Bitcoin, Ethereum, macro economics, and traditional markets.

### Key Features

**Advanced Analytics**
Access sophisticated on-chain metrics, macro indicators, and proprietary quantitative models designed for professional investors.

**Real-Time Data**
Monitor market conditions with up-to-the-minute data feeds and customizable alert systems.

**Strategic Intelligence**
Leverage machine learning models and quantitative strategies actively used by the Capriole Fund.

**Risk Management**
Utilize Guardian risk metrics and comprehensive Bitcoin treasury company analysis for informed decision-making.
