## Build with Capriole AI

Capriole AI is a standalone AI relay and API product for developers, automation workflows, and production integrations. It gives you an OpenAI-compatible surface for model discovery and chat completions, with public pricing designed to stay clear and easy to compare.

- One API surface for model discovery and chat completions
- Final public prices without route complexity
- Savings versus official pricing on selected models
- A lightweight fit for teams that want a straightforward integration path
